Research Experience

  1. After reading a lot of books on the 2008 financial crisis, I have concluded that the main cause of the financial crisis and the subsequent economic weakness was the over-expansion of debt. However, many countries, such as China, are currently relying on debt expansion to drive their economies. Therefore, finding a way of economic development that is not dependent on debt may make it possible to avoid similar economic crises.

  2. Examines the impact of different events such as earnings announcements, management changes and policy and regulatory adjustments on the share price of FTSE 300 company Meggitt. I find that events that theoretically would have an impact on the share price are not significant in the empirical evidence. This could be due to the fact that the theoretical analysis is based on rationality assumptions and simplified models, while the empirical results are affected by multiple factors such as market complexity and investor sentiment.
